Bid Price Modeling

Optimization is performed by building and then using Bid Price Models. These models are developed based on the data derived from the DMP and on the results of A/B testing experiments.

Bid price is the optimal value a brand should pay for the impression to maximize incremental profit from it.


Bid price modeling contains two major components:

User value modeling including

  • User's predicted probability to convert once on the Marketer's site
  • User's predicted spend with the Marketer

and User response modeling involving

  • Marketer-specific probability of user's response to impressions with various combinations of features
  • Results of A/B testing experiments
