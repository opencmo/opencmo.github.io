Algorithmic Bidder

In contrast to other platforms, OpenDSP clients' strategy and execution are seamlessly integrated within the client's Bidder. Instead of being defined offline, within the user interface, the strategy is determined by the decision engine in real time by applying algorithms that compute the bid price for every bid request. The real-time algorithmic strategy is focused on Optimization to Marketer's incremental Profit.

 

Bid price models are developed to optimize towards your KPI's, most notably incremental profit and true marketing ROI. Bid price derived from these models is optimal for the marketer and guarantees incremental profit from the advertising campaign. User data contained in your integrated DMP and the results of A/B testing experiments are used to build these models. 

 

The bidder processes bid request and augments it in real time with such data as:

  • relevant user segments derived from the DMP;
  • relevant page, temporal, location, frequency and other features derived from multiple accessible data sources;
  • high-level campaign parameters, constraints, and filters set up by campaign managers in the OpenDSP User Interface.

 

Using augmented bid request data as an input into the bid price model, the decision engine computes the optimal bid price and selects the optimal creative for every impression opportunity. Execution ends with serving an ad if the impression is won. Every step of the execution process is governed by the client's real-time decisions, and the outcome of every step is completely transparent.

 

Universal anti-fraud technology scans every potential impression placement and runs it through a multitude of sofisticated filters to prevent bidding on more than 98% of fraudulent impressions. It is also customizable to fit individual needs of every brand.
