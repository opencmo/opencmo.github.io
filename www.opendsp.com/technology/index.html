Platform Capabilities

OpenDSP has all the usual capabilities of a standard DSP platform, such as access to RTB traffic (Display, Video, Mobile), proprietary ad server, and a full-scale user interface. It is integrated with multiple ad exchanges and data providers whose services can be used for targeting and filtering. OpenDSP gives users access to insightful analytics and real-time reporting on all measurable ad campaign parameters. 

As a comprehensive SaaS platform, OpenDSP also offers a number of distinct advantages over standard DSP services:

 

  • Client's ownership of the entire AdTech stack with full customization capability and complete transparency
  • Algorithmic bidding optimized to marketer's incremental profit (custom Bidders)
  • Novel scalable A/B testing experiments
  • Access to multiple data sources for analytics and modeling

 

  • The most efficient cost structure - determined by the amount of infrastructure needed and data usage
  • No media markup charge




OpenDSP also provides additional services, such as support, analytics, data science and creative development, all billed at reasonable consulting rates.
