A/B Testing Experiments

A/B testing is the only way to measure the effectiveness of an advertising campaign. By discovering users who respond to ads and means to optimize their response, A/B testing experiments can also help optimize campaign ROI. OpenDSP offers marketers a groundbreaking technology for A/B testing experiments.

Take advantage of continuous, scalable A/B testing experiments that are conducted at no cost and require no media spend on PSA's. OpenDSP allows clients to automatically carry out multiple experiments on many user segments and impression features at the same time. Clients can also manually set up experiments to test specific user segments and impression features, such as:

  • Contextual category
  • Viewability of the webpage and its predicted ad in-view time
  • Time of the day and day of the week
  • Physical location
  • Ad size and position
  • Number and frequency of impressions

 

Start running A/B testing with an ad campaign, and the platform will transmit real-time feedback from the experiments into the bidder's decision engine. Bid price models are tuned automatically, resulting in the optimization of the effectiveness of targeting selected user segments in many different ways at the same time. This allows marketers to continuously increase their incremental revenue and true marketing ROI.
