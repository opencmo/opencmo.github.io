Ability To Manage Digital Media With DSP

Posted at December 21, 2010

Demand Side Platforms are being used by advertisers because of their ability to manage media and automate bids in real time while using only one interface. Perhaps the most appealing aspect of using a DSP is that it is a more simple way to manage real time bidding. Agencies are able to more easily choose how and where their ads are being placed. At the same time, they are able to utilize many inventory sources at once, and this allows for better performance and ROI from campaigns. Most DSP’s also will have built in campaign management tools which allow the user to either automatically target or individually (manually) target with their ads. This also allows for better time-management.

The benefit to DSP’s and their real time bidding technology lies in the convenience. With the use of a demand side platform, there is no need to log-in to different servers and systems for every provider an advertiser is working with. Most DSP’s have the ability to connect with any Ad Server. Some have compared the efficiency of demand side platforms to that of an SEM campaign, all while having the transparency and manageability advertisers want and need.

Viewing an audience and evaluating inventory on a large scale allows users of DSP’s to track behavioral tendencies and retarget accordingly. This is because more of the internet exchanges are visible in one place, and the more opportunities you have to target, the more easily you may be able to judge value on a specific campaign or amount of impressions. The result: an increase in ROI.

Some DSP’s also allow for you to set a budget and control campaigns on a much larger scale. This eliminates the need for micro-managing media across many engines and ad serving vehicles. On top of this, DSP reporting is generally more easily evaluated because all data and results can be viewed on one program.

DSP’s are slowly replacing more traditional ad serving ventures as they enable users to save money, increase ROI and more easily manage their campaigns.
